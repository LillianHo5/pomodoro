Instructions

Work Sessions

To start a work session, click on the Pomodoro button. You can adjust the amount of time you would like to work in a given interval by clicking the + and - buttons!

Breaks

Congratulations! After completing a work session, you are given the option to take a short break, which is 5 minutes long, before starting another work session. During this time, be sure to drink a glass of water or stretch! In order to take a long break, you must complete 4 sessions.

Keeping track of time focused

You can keep track of how long you have stayed focused for everytime you have completed a work session. In order to save your progress, do not close this tab or refresh the page!
